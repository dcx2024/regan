Kemi Lawal

Vice President

Oluwakemi Adetutu Lawal, born April 20, 1973, is a passionate advocate for the environment with a background in Physics from the University of Ibadan. She began her career as a flight attendant, traveling globally for a decade before returning to her first love—gardening. Now an entrepreneur in health, wellness, and regenerative agriculture, Kemi also serves in environmental public service and is a committed Rotarian known for her dedication to sustainability and community service.

Mary Akoh

Public relations Officer

Mary Akoh, born on May 1, 1973, attended Our Lady of Apostles Primary School (1979–1985) and Reagan Memorial Baptist Girls’ Secondary School, Yaba (1985–1991). She earned a B.Sc. in Sociology from the University of Lagos and an MPA from Lagos State University (2004–2006). A member of the Association of Enterprise Risk Management Professionals since 2017, she is married with four children.

Olanrewaju Abe

Olubunmi Adetinuke Adeleke

Welfare Officer

Olubunmi Adetinuke Adeleke, born on September 28, 1973, in Lagos, attended Our Lady of Apostle Primary School and Reagan Memorial Baptist Girls’ School, Yaba. She earned a degree in Social Studies from Ekiti State University (1995–1999). Married to Mr. Adewale Adeleke, they have six children—three biological and three adopted. A successful caterer and event planner, Olubunmi is known for her strong organizational and interpersonal skills, as well as her compassionate, nurturing approach.

Meet our Electoral Team

Dorcas Olayinka Agbaje

Electoral Chairlady

Dorcas Olayinka Agbaje, born on September 26, 1974, holds a degree in Geography from Benue State University and diplomas in Secretarial Administration and Business Studies from UK institutions. She later earned a distinction in Psychiatric Nursing from Brandon University, Canada, and is a registered psychiatric nurse in Manitoba. She is a dedicated professional and single mother of one.

Oluwafemi Fatokun

Electoral Commitee

Oluwafemi Fatokun (née Ojomo), born on April 25, attended the University of Lagos Staff School and is a proud alumna of Reagan Memorial Baptist Girls’ School, Class of 1991. She holds a B.Sc. in Banking and Finance from EKSU (formerly OSUA). After gaining experience in the finance sector, she transitioned into the textile industry, where she has thrived as a dedicated merchant for the past 18 years. She is married and blessed with children.

Tolulope Osoba

Committee Member

Tolulope Osoba, born on November 19, attended Army Children School and Reagan Memorial Baptist Girls’ Secondary School (1985–1991). She holds a degree in Psychology from Ondo State University and a Master’s in Social Work from Lagos State University, with certification in Medical/Psychiatric Social Work. A certified Hotline Master Trainer and International Volunteer, Tolulope is a fellow of WSLA (Chichester) and SLDP (Nigeria). She enjoys music, travel, dance, and martial arts..
